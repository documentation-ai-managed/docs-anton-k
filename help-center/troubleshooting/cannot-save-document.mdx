## Issue

- Trying to save a document gives an error
- Changes do not appear after refresh

## Common Causes

- Network connection lost
- Session expired

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    Refresh the page and make sure you are connected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log out and log back in">
    Session issues are often fixed by re-authenticating.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support if problem persists">
    Email support@antonk.com with error details.
  </Step>
</Steps>
