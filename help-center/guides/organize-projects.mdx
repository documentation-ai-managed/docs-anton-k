---
title: Organize Documentation Projects
description: Simple steps to organize your Anton K projects.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create folders">
    Use the "New Folder" button to group related docs together.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drag and drop documents">
    Move pages into folders by dragging them in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename and reorder">
    Click the three dots on any item to rename or change its position.
  </Step>
</Steps>
