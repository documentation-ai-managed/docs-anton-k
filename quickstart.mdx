## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account to import projects later

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Visit [https://antonk.com/signup](https://antonk.com/signup) to get started.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email Signup" icon="mail">

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google OAuth" icon="apple">
    Click "Sign up with Google" for instant access without a new password.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Once logged in, you land on the dashboard at [https://dashboard.antonk.com](https://dashboard.antonk.com).

## Set Up Your First Documentation Space

Follow these steps to create a space and add content.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Space" icon="plus">
    Click the **New Space** button in the top-right corner of your dashboard.

    Enter a name like "My Project Docs" and select a brand color (e.g., `#3B82F6` for Anton K blue).

    Click **Create Space**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Your First Project" icon="git-branch">
    Inside your new space, select **Add Project**.

    Choose **Blank Project** for starters, or **Import from GitHub** if you have a repo ready.

    Name it "Quickstart Project" and save.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your First Page" icon="file-text">
    Navigate to your project and click **New Page**.

    Title it "Introduction" and use the MDX editor to add content.

    Here's a starter example:
    
````mdx
```javascript
console.log("Welcome to Anton K!");
```
````
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish and Preview" icon="eye">
    Click **Publish** to make your page live.

    Use the preview pane to verify rendering, including code blocks and components.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip" kind="success">
  Your first page is now live! Share the URL from the address bar with your team.
</Callout>
